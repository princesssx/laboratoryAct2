Day VI — Tough Day

Setback happens, but so does growth ✧

♡

D-day for our Manila Trip. I left my boarding house at 2AM because my brother decided to travel early so we could arrive early in Manila.

However, things do not always go with the plan. Some accidents happened and one of that was one of the wheels of our car popped and got flat. We asked for help from the locals and luckily it was easily solved.

I also cried this day because I was not able to submit my homework for my major subject on time. I was so disappointed with myself. But I also thought that it's just a bad day and mistakes can happen. A reminder for myself that I can always make "bawi" for these things.
