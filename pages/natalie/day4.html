Day IV — Spontaneous Break

Unexpected free time turns into unforgettable plans ✧

♡

The second quiz got cancelled which lead to sudden planning of our outing. I initiated the plan for us to bond and let our minds relax after studying for that two quiz that got called off. Our group chat became active and we got excited by the thought of it.

I asked for my brother's permission since I also wanted to let them know my whereabouts. Fortunately, my brother gave me his consent and the planning went on.

Some of our friends could not make it since their parents did not agree. Unfortunately, we need to proceed without them.
